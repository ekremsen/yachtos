# Finance Module

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Document Owner:** Product Team  
**Last Updated:** 29 June 2026

---

## Purpose

The Finance module manages the financial operations of a yacht.

It provides visibility into operational expenses, budgets and financial history while maintaining complete traceability.

The Finance module focuses on yacht operations rather than accounting.

---

## Business Goal

Provide captains and yacht owners with a centralized financial management system that simplifies expense tracking and budget monitoring.

---

## Responsibilities

The Finance module is responsible for:

- Expenses
- Expense categories
- Budget management
- Financial history
- Receipt management
- Expense approvals
- Budget monitoring

The Finance module is **not** responsible for:

- Payroll
- Tax calculations
- Accounting
- Banking integrations
- Supplier management

These responsibilities belong to their respective modules.

---

## Initial Scope

The MVP should allow users to:

- Record expenses
- Categorize expenses
- Upload receipts
- Monitor budgets
- View financial history

---

## Out of Scope

The MVP will not include:

- Accounting software integrations
- Invoice generation
- Tax reporting
- Bank integrations
- Multi-currency accounting

These capabilities may be introduced in future versions.

---

## Success Criteria

Captains can accurately record operational expenses, monitor yacht budgets and provide transparent financial reporting to yacht owners.
