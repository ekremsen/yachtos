# Yacht Module

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Document Owner:** Product Team  
**Last Updated:** 29 June 2026

---

## Purpose

The Yacht module is the core business domain of YachtOS.

Every operational activity within the platform is centered around a yacht.

A yacht serves as the primary operational workspace where captains, crew members, maintenance activities, inventory, guests and documents come together.

---

## Business Goal

Provide a centralized and structured digital representation of a yacht and its daily operations.

The Yacht module acts as the foundation for all operational workflows.

---

## Responsibilities

The Yacht module is responsible for:

- Yacht information
- Yacht specifications
- Operational status
- Yacht lifecycle
- Yacht ownership history
- Yacht assignments

The Yacht module is **not** responsible for:

- Crew management
- Maintenance management
- Inventory management
- Financial management
- Charter management

These responsibilities belong to their respective modules.

---

## Initial Scope

The MVP should allow users to:

- Create a yacht
- Update yacht information
- Archive a yacht
- View yacht details
- View assigned users

---

## Out of Scope

The MVP will not include:

- Yacht performance analytics
- IoT integrations
- Live vessel tracking
- Engine telemetry
- Fuel monitoring

These capabilities may be introduced in future versions.

---

## Success Criteria

A captain or owner can create and manage a yacht as the operational center of YachtOS.

Every other operational module should naturally connect to the yacht.
