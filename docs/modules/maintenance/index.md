# Maintenance Module

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Document Owner:** Product Team  
**Last Updated:** 29 June 2026

---

## Purpose

The Maintenance module manages all planned, preventive and corrective maintenance activities for yachts.

It ensures that maintenance operations are scheduled, executed and documented in a consistent and auditable manner.

---

## Business Goal

Provide a centralized maintenance management system that helps captains keep yachts operational, compliant and ready for use.

---

## Responsibilities

The Maintenance module is responsible for:

- Maintenance planning
- Preventive maintenance
- Corrective maintenance
- Maintenance history
- Maintenance schedules
- Maintenance status
- Maintenance attachments

The Maintenance module is **not** responsible for:

- Inventory management
- Financial accounting
- Document management
- User management

These responsibilities belong to their respective modules.

---

## Initial Scope

The MVP should allow users to:

- Create maintenance records
- Schedule maintenance
- Update maintenance status
- Complete maintenance tasks
- View maintenance history

---

## Out of Scope

The MVP will not include:

- Predictive maintenance
- IoT integrations
- Engine telemetry
- Automatic maintenance scheduling
- Third-party maintenance integrations

These capabilities may be introduced in future versions.

---

## Success Criteria

Captains can manage all yacht maintenance activities from a single module while maintaining a complete maintenance history.
