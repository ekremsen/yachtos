# Inventory Module

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Document Owner:** Product Team  
**Last Updated:** 29 June 2026

---

## Purpose

The Inventory module manages all physical assets, consumables and onboard supplies stored on a yacht.

It provides real-time visibility into stock levels, movements and replenishment needs.

---

## Business Goal

Provide captains with a reliable inventory management system that prevents shortages, improves operational readiness and maintains accurate stock records.

---

## Responsibilities

The Inventory module is responsible for:

- Inventory items
- Stock quantities
- Stock movements
- Consumption records
- Replenishment requests
- Inventory categories
- Storage locations
- Low stock monitoring

The Inventory module is **not** responsible for:

- Purchasing
- Financial accounting
- Maintenance planning
- Guest management

These responsibilities belong to their respective modules.

---

## Initial Scope

The MVP should allow users to:

- Create inventory items
- Update stock quantities
- Record stock movements
- View inventory history
- Monitor low stock levels

---

## Out of Scope

The MVP will not include:

- Barcode scanning
- RFID integration
- Supplier management
- Purchase orders
- Automatic replenishment

These capabilities may be introduced in future versions.

---

## Success Criteria

Captains can accurately track onboard inventory, identify shortages before they become operational problems and maintain complete stock history.
