# Charter Module

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Document Owner:** Product Team  
**Last Updated:** 29 June 2026

---

## Purpose

The Charter module manages commercial yacht charter operations.

It provides tools for managing charter bookings, trips, guests and commercial workflows while integrating seamlessly with the operational modules of YachtOS.

The Charter module extends YachtOS without changing the core operational architecture.

---

## Business Goal

Provide charter companies with a complete charter management solution while reusing the same operational foundation used by private yacht owners.

---

## Responsibilities

The Charter module is responsible for:

- Charter bookings
- Charter schedules
- Guest lists
- Charter trips
- Charter contracts
- Charter pricing
- Charter payments
- Charter history

The Charter module is **not** responsible for:

- Yacht management
- Crew management
- Inventory management
- Maintenance management

These responsibilities belong to their respective modules.

---

## Initial Scope

The MVP will include only the architectural foundation required for future charter support.

Commercial charter workflows will not be implemented during the MVP.

---

## Out of Scope

The MVP will not include:

- Online booking
- Availability calendar
- Pricing engine
- Contract generation
- Payment processing
- Customer portal
- Travel agency integrations

These capabilities are planned for future versions.

---

## Success Criteria

The YachtOS architecture supports charter companies without requiring structural changes to the existing operational modules.
