## Purpose

YachtOS is a SaaS platform built to simplify the daily operational responsibilities of luxury yacht captains.

Luxury yacht owners expect their vessels to be ready, safe, compliant and comfortable whenever they need them. In practice, this responsibility is carried by captains.

Today, many captains manage yacht operations through Excel files, WhatsApp messages, phone calls, emails, paper notes and personal memory.

YachtOS centralizes this operational knowledge into a structured, searchable and collaborative platform.

The goal is simple:

> Help captains remember less, organize more and operate with confidence.

---

## Product Vision

To become the operational workspace for luxury yacht captains worldwide.

YachtOS is not only a yacht management system. It is designed to become the digital memory and daily command center of a luxury yacht operation.

---

## Primary User

The primary user is the **Captain**.

Captains are responsible for daily operations, maintenance coordination, inventory readiness, crew organization, guest expectations and communication with yacht owners.

The product must always be designed with captain productivity as the first priority.

---

## Economic Buyer

The economic buyer is usually the **Yacht Owner**.

However, in many real-world yacht operations, owners allocate an annual budget and captains manage how that budget is used for the benefit of the yacht.

For this reason, the captain is both the daily user and an important operational decision maker.

---

## Core Problem

Luxury yacht operations depend heavily on fragmented information.

Common tools currently used:

- Excel spreadsheets
- WhatsApp messages
- Phone calls
- Emails
- Paper documents
- Personal memory

This creates several risks:

- Important maintenance tasks may be forgotten.
- Inventory needs may be missed.
- Guest preferences may not be remembered.
- Crew and certificate information may become hard to track.
- Owners may not have clear visibility.
- Captains carry unnecessary mental load.

---

## Real-World Example

A yacht owner may call the captain and request a specific item, such as a 25-year-old Chivas whisky.

The owner does not want to check inventory.

The owner expects the captain to already know whether the item is available, should be purchased or was requested before.

The same applies to guest preferences, allergies, special requests, maintenance needs, documents and operational tasks.

YachtOS exists to make this operational memory reliable.

---

## Target Market

The initial target market is:

- Single yacht owners
- Captains managing private luxury yachts

Future target markets may include:

- Charter companies
- Fleet management companies
- Yacht management agencies
- Marina operators

The MVP will focus on captains first.

---

## Product Positioning

YachtOS is the operational workspace for luxury yacht captains.

It replaces scattered spreadsheets, conversations and memory-based tracking with one structured platform.

---

## Key Value Propositions

For captains:

- Less mental load
- Better organization
- Faster access to operational information
- Task and maintenance visibility
- Inventory awareness
- Guest preference tracking
- Mobile access from anywhere

For yacht owners:

- More confidence
- Better visibility
- Operational reliability
- Reduced dependency on informal communication
- Clearer overview of yacht readiness

---

## Differentiation

YachtOS will compete primarily with Excel, WhatsApp and manual processes.

The product must win through:

- Ease of use
- Modern interface
- Mobile-first accessibility
- Notification system
- Operational clarity
- Captain-first product experience

---

## Product Validation

YachtOS is being shaped with input from experienced yacht captains.

The first design partner is a captain with access to a wider captain network in Türkiye.

This gives YachtOS an opportunity to validate real operational problems before building unnecessary features.

---

## Product Principle

No feature should be built without validation.

Every feature must answer:

- What real problem does this solve?
- Who experiences this problem?
- How is it solved today?
- Why is YachtOS a better solution?
- Is this needed for the MVP?

---

## Non-Goals For MVP

YachtOS MVP will not attempt to become:

- A full accounting system
- A public charter marketplace
- A marina management system
- An IoT monitoring platform
- A complex ERP system

The MVP will focus on operational clarity for captains.
