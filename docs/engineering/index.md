# Engineering Standards

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Document Owner:** Engineering Team  
**Last Updated:** 29 June 2026

---

## Purpose

This document defines the engineering principles used throughout YachtOS.

Its purpose is to ensure consistency, maintainability and long-term scalability.

---

## Core Principles

- Documentation before implementation.
- API First.
- Modular Monolith architecture.
- Security by Design.
- Multi-Tenant by Design.
- Readability over cleverness.
- Consistency over preference.
- Complexity must be earned.

---

## Documentation Rule

No implementation should begin before its documentation is approved.

---

## Development Rule

Every feature must answer:

- Why does it exist?
- Who needs it?
- What business value does it provide?
- Can it be implemented more simply?

---

## Coding Philosophy

- Write readable code.
- Prefer explicit solutions.
- Avoid premature optimization.
- Avoid unnecessary abstractions.
- Business logic never belongs in controllers.

---

## Product Philosophy

Every technical decision should support the business goals of YachtOS.

Technology exists to solve business problems, not to showcase engineering.
