# YachtOS Vision

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Document Owner:** Product Team  
**Last Updated:** 29 June 2026

---

## Vision Statement

YachtOS aims to become the operational workspace for luxury yacht captains worldwide.

The long-term vision is to transform yacht operations from memory-based, fragmented and reactive work into structured, visible and reliable operational management.

---

## Long-Term Direction

YachtOS is not intended to be a simple checklist application.

The product should evolve into a platform where captains can manage:

- Daily operations
- Maintenance readiness
- Inventory availability
- Guest preferences
- Crew responsibilities
- Yacht documents
- Owner expectations
- Operational history

from one central system.

---

## Product Belief

Luxury yacht operations are only successful when small details are remembered consistently.

YachtOS exists because operational excellence depends on memory, structure and accountability.

The product should help captains reduce mental load while increasing operational confidence.

---

## Strategic Focus

The first strategic focus is captains managing private luxury yachts.

Future expansion may include:

- Charter companies
- Fleet operators
- Yacht management agencies
- Marina-related operations

However, the product must first solve the daily operational pain of captains.

---

## Five-Year Vision

Within five years, YachtOS should become a trusted operational platform used by captains, owners and yacht management companies in multiple countries.

The platform should support:

- Multi-language usage
- Multi-currency operations
- Multi-timezone operations
- Mobile-first workflows
- Role-based access
- Operational reporting
- AI-assisted recommendations

---

## Vision Principle

Build for the next 10 years, deliver for the next 3 months.

YachtOS must always balance long-term architectural quality with short-term product delivery.
