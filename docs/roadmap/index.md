# Roadmap

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Document Owner:** Product Team  
**Last Updated:** 29 June 2026

---

## Purpose

This document defines the high-level product roadmap of YachtOS.

The roadmap explains how the product should evolve from the first MVP to future versions.

It is not a task list.

It is a strategic direction document.

---

## Roadmap Principle

YachtOS will be developed with a captain-first approach.

The product must first solve the daily operational pain of captains before expanding into broader yacht management, charter operations or enterprise fleet features.

---

## Development Strategy

YachtOS will evolve in three major stages:

1. MVP
2. Version 1
3. Future Expansion

Each stage must deliver clear business value before the next stage begins.

---

## MVP Focus

The MVP must prove that captains can manage core yacht operations better with YachtOS than with Excel, WhatsApp and personal memory.

The MVP should focus on:

- Yacht setup
- Captain onboarding
- Crew records
- Task management
- Maintenance tracking
- Inventory awareness
- Guest preferences
- Basic notifications

---

## Version 1 Focus

Version 1 should improve operational depth and owner visibility.

This stage may include:

- Owner dashboard
- Advanced reporting
- Document management
- Budget tracking
- Approval flows
- Mobile-first improvements
- Better notification rules

---

## Future Focus

Future versions may expand YachtOS into a broader yacht operation ecosystem.

Possible directions include:

- Charter company support
- Fleet management
- AI-assisted recommendations
- Supplier integrations
- Marina integrations
- Multi-country commercial expansion
- Advanced analytics

---

## Roadmap Rule

No feature should move into development without validation.

Each feature must answer:

- Which user needs this?
- What problem does it solve?
- How is this handled today?
- Why should YachtOS solve it?
- Is it required for the current roadmap stage?
